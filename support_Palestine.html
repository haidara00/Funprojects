Direct Humanitarian Support for Palestine

This page serves as an information hub to connect donors with established and vetted humanitarian organizations providing urgent aid in Gaza and the West Bank.

Important: This site does not collect or process donations. It directs you to the official, secure donation portals of the organizations listed.

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Notice for Donors

Due to strict international sanctions and financial regulations, direct peer-to-peer fundraising for individuals in Gaza via major platforms carries a high risk of funds being frozen, returned, or accounts shut down[citation:2][citation:10].

Donating through legally authorized and registered non-governmental organizations (NGOs) is the safest and most reliable method to ensure your aid reaches those in need[citation:8].

Vetted Humanitarian Organizations

The following organizations are recognized for their humanitarian work in the region. Always conduct your own due diligence before donating.

  • Palestine Children's Relief Fund (PCRF) Provides medical care and humanitarian aid to sick and injured children. Visit PCRF
  • Sawaed Palestine A platform focused on providing basic needs like food, tents, and medical support in Gaza[citation:6]. Visit Sawaed Palestine
  • UNRWA (UN Relief and Works Agency) Provides education, healthcare, and relief services to Palestinian refugees. Visit UNRWA

Note: This is a shortlist for demonstration. Extensive research is required to identify the most suitable and current partners.
